# Writing Samples Overview

This folder contains selected writing samples developed as part of coursework in AI and healthcare. These materials demonstrate the ability to analyze clinical concepts, data quality challenges, ethical considerations, and system-level implications of artificial intelligence in healthcare.

Featured work includes:
- A formal academic paper analyzing medical terminology as a foundation for AI-enabled healthcare
- Reflective writing connecting clinical language to data quality, bias, and trust
- Structured technical notes designed for mixed clinical, technical, and policy audiences

Together, these samples illustrate clear written communication, critical thinking, and an applied understanding of how AI systems intersect with real-world healthcare environments.
